Site Purpose

This site exists to bring local businesses to the attention of tourists and potential investors. It makes it easy for tourists to engage with local businesses and for investors to know exactly what local businesses are all about. We aim to provide three main services: One is to serve as a constantly updated directory containing the names, contact details, and addresses of all local businesses. Another objective is to provide information on the different promos and events hosted by different businesses at any given time. Finally, we aim to make public the successes and maybe even the challenges that some businesses may be encountering, while sharing what the city is doing to be more conducive to commerce.
